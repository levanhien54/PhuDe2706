====================================================================
  VIDEO DUBBING — BAT DAU NHANH (Quick Start)
====================================================================

Ung dung long tieng video bang AI, chay HOAN TOAN OFFLINE tren may
ban (khong can Internet sau khi cai xong). Can may Windows co GPU
NVIDIA rieng (xem yeu cau chi tiet trong file 01-CAI-DAT.md).

--------------------------------------------------------------------
5 BUOC DE CHAY UNG DUNG
--------------------------------------------------------------------

BUOC 1 — Chep 2 file vao CUNG MOT thu muc
    Setup.exe
    app.7z
    (Hai file nay PHAI nam chung mot thu muc, khong duoc tach rieng
    — Setup.exe can tim thay app.7z ben canh no de giai nen.)

BUOC 2 — Chay Setup.exe
    Nhay dup vao Setup.exe, chon noi cai dat (mac dinh:
    %LOCALAPPDATA%\VideoDubbing), bam cai dat va cho giai nen xong.
    ~ Thoi gian du kien: vai phut (tuy toc do o dia — bo giai nen
      chua khoang 20 GB).

BUOC 3 — Kiem tra he thong
    Sau khi cai xong, tren Desktop / Start Menu se co shortcut
    "Video Dubbing". Trong thu muc cai dat cung co file
    Kiem-tra-he-thong.bat — nhay dup vao do CHAY TRUOC KHI dung app.
    Doc ket qua hien ra: moi dong phai la [PASS] (dat yeu cau).
    Neu co dong [FAIL] mau do, xem file 01-CAI-DAT.md de biet cach
    xu ly truoc khi sang buoc 4.
    ~ Thoi gian du kien: vai giay.

BUOC 4 — Mo "Video Dubbing"
    Nhay dup vao shortcut "Video Dubbing" (Desktop hoac Start Menu).

BUOC 5 — Doi man hinh khoi dong bao du dich vu
    Mot cua so nho hien ra voi danh sach 5 dong trang thai:
        GPU · Dich (Ollama) · Nhan giong · Giong doc · Dieu phoi
    Doi den khi CA 5 DONG deu co dau check (tich xanh). Sau do cua
    so chinh cua ung dung se tu mo (trinh duyet noi bo / cua so
    Electron).
    ~ Thoi gian du kien: khoang 1-2 phut cho lan khoi dong dau tien
      (nap model vao GPU — WhisperX thuong lau nhat).

--------------------------------------------------------------------
SAU KHI MO DUOC UNG DUNG
--------------------------------------------------------------------
- Keo-tha video vao khung "Tai Video Len", hoac bo video vao thu
  muc data\input trong thu muc cai dat.
- Chon cau hinh (ngon ngu dich, giong doc, khop moi, xoa chu...).
- Bam "Bat dau long tieng" va theo doi tien trinh.
- Video hoan thanh nam trong thu muc data\output (co the doi noi
  luu trong "Cau hinh" > "Thu muc luu video").

Chi tiet day du: xem 01-CAI-DAT.md, 02-SU-DUNG.md va
03-XU-LY-SU-CO.md trong cung thu muc HUONG-DAN nay.
